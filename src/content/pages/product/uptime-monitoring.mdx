Our probes are deployed on Fly.io, Koyeb and Railway. We have deployed it in 3 different regions to give you the opportunity to test it from cloud providers that might not be the one you are currently using.

## Why uptime monitoring is important?

Uptime monitoring is your first line of defense to ensure your service is available for your customers. By monitoring your service from multiple regions around the world, you can be sure that your customers are able to reach your service.

## Why choose openstatus for uptime monitoring?

### Global Monitoring

You can monitor your services from multiple regions around the world.

Check all regions in the [global speed checker](/landing/play/checker).

### Multi Cloud

Our probes are deployed on multiple providers like **Fly.io**, **Koyeb** and **Railway**, each having multiple regions.

### CLI

You can manage or trigger your monitors via CLI. We provide a simple [GitHub Action](https://github.com/marketplace/actions/openstatus-synthetics-ci) to run it in your **CI/CD**.

### Monitoring as code

The best way to **version** your monitor changes is via the openstatus CLI and its **`YAML` config**. Additionally, we provide you a [Terraform](https://registry.terraform.io/providers/openstatusHQ/openstatus/latest). Read more [about monitoring as code](/landing/monitoring-as-code)

### Status Pages

Automatically update your status page based on your uptime monitoring results. Push reports and communicate with your users during incidents. Read more [about status pages](/landing/status-page).
